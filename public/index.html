Descriptive Statistics

In this series , I'm going to go through some basic concepts of statistics required in Data Science. There are two main branches of statistics : descriptive statistics tells us the statistics about data like mean, mode and standard deviation, which you've learned in high school. inferential statistics, on the other hand, uses a random dataset sampled from population to make inferences about population. They are totally two different things.
